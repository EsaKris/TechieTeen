About TechieTeen

Who We Are

TechieTeen is a flagship program powered by the Learning Arm of Techiekraft Edtech company. Designed for high school students, it’s a live, interactive session that introduces them to the boundless possibilities within the technology ecosystem.

The initiative equips students with knowledge about various fields in digital technology while helping them identify and develop the skills needed to excel in these areas. By positioning learners early in their journey, TechieTeen fosters the next generation of innovators ready to lead in the digital era.

Why TechieTeen?

TechieTeen stands out as a one-of-a-kind program that combines education, empowerment, and opportunity:

Live Engagement

Unlike conventional programs, TechieTeen provides a hands-on, live interaction that ensures students actively engage with real-world insights.

Exposure to Opportunities

TechieTeen connects students and schools with globally recognized partners like Lampnets Solutions, School360 LMS, and Winumu creating access to scholarships, tools, and resources that enhance learning outcomes.

Early Preparation for the Future

By exposing students to digital technology early, TechieTeen gives them a significant advantage in pursuing tech-related careers and becoming the inventors the future demands.

Global Recognition

Partnering with internationally acclaimed organizations, TechieTeen is recognized and accredited, ensuring credibility and quality.

What to Expect from TechieTeen

For Students

  • Insightful Conversations: Interactive sessions with industry professionals to inspire and guide students.
  • Visual Learning: Dynamic presentations and practical demonstrations to deepen understanding of tech concepts.
  • Scholarship Opportunities: Access to scholarships and incentives provided by TechieTeen’s global partners.

For Hosting Schools

  • Financial Management Tools: Free software tools for managing school finances effectively.
  • Offline CBT Applications: Access to offline Computer-Based Testing systems for smoother assessments.
  • Results Management Solutions: Tools for efficient online and offline management of student results.
  • Enhanced Online Presence: Hosting schools benefit from tools and resources to boost their visibility and credibility online.
